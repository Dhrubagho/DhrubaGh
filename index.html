About Us

Welcome to Plantvex! My name is Dhruba Das, and I created this website to combine my passion for plants with my journey in web development. As a Botany student, I'm fascinated by the plant world, and I wanted to build something that could help others discover and appreciate it too.

Plantvex is a place where I share what I've learned about various plants, including scientific names, local names, and key information about each species. It’s a personal project that allows me to dive deeper into botany while also practicing web development skills like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. All images featured on this site are sourced from Pixabay, a platform offering high-quality, royalty-free visuals.

My goal with Plantvex is to create a resource that’s both informative and accessible, whether you’re a fellow student, a plant enthusiast, or someone just curious about the natural world. Building this website has been a rewarding way to bring together my studies and my technical interests.

Thank you for visiting and being part of my journey. I hope you find Plantvex both useful and enjoyable as I continue to expand its content and features!
